About our zoo

Welcome to Zoopernatural, the world’s only zoological sanctuary where the laws of nature politely step aside. Nestled in a hidden valley perpetually wrapped in twilight fog, this otherworldly reserve is home to beings both magnificent and mildly alarming.
Visitors enter through wrought-iron gates humming with warding spells, where spectral peacocks fan their translucent tails in greeting. The habitats shift subtly as you walk — a shimmering tundra one moment, a pocket jungle dripping with luminous vines the next.

Zoopernatural’s keepers are as curious as their charges — part biologist, part exorcist, all slightly sleep-deprived. Between their rounds, they bottle ambient magic, maintain the dimensional fences, and politely request that no one feed the gremlins after moonset.
At dusk, a soft bell tolls, signaling the Ethereal Parade, when creatures roam freely through the park, phasing between realities as lanterns of captured starlight guide them home.

Dining in Zoopernatural

Visitors of the Zoopernatural can take a break from spectral encounters and enjoy a selection of eerie yet inviting dining locations. Each restaurant offers a unique atmosphere inspired by the entities nearby, blending the supernatural with surprisingly comforting cuisine.


  • The Wailing Bistro
    Located near the Banshee enclosure, this dimly lit restaurant features soft echoes of distant wails. Guests enjoy warm meals in a hauntingly elegant setting.

  • Phantom Feast Hall
    Surrounded by drifting apparitions, this grand hall serves classic dishes with a ghostly presentation that seems to fade and reappear before your eyes.

  • The Misty Lantern Café
    Nestled beside the Will-o’-the-Wisp habitat, this quiet café glows with floating lights, offering teas, pastries, and a calm escape from the darker exhibits.

Whether seeking comfort or curiosity, these dining spots ensure that even in a world of spirits and shadows, no visitor goes hungry.

Welcome to the Zoopernatural

Explore a world beyond the ordinary, where mysterious presences and unexplained phenomena await. Wander through dimly lit paths and discover unique exhibits filled with strange energy, soft whispers, and flickers of movement just beyond sight.


  • Echoing Chambers – Experience repeating moments frozen in time.

  • Kinetic Hall – Watch objects move in ways that defy logic.

  • Lantern Marsh – Follow drifting lights through a misty landscape.

  • Shadow Gallery – See darkness shift and take on a life of its own.

  • Silent Hall – A space filled with distant, haunting sounds.

  • Energy Grid – Observe powerful unseen forces at work.

  • Spectral Grounds – Glimpses of familiar forms, not quite of this world.

  • Reflection Zone – Encounters that mirror your movements.

  • Veil Room – Shapes that fade in and out of view.

  • Dream Wing – A calm area where thoughts and imagination drift freely.

A place of wonder, curiosity, and mystery—welcome, and enjoy your visit.

Playground in the Zoopernatural

Younger visitors can enjoy a safe and magical playground designed with gentle supernatural themes. The area blends playful activities with glowing lights, soft shadows, and harmless ghostly effects.

  • Ghost Bounce Zone
    A soft play area where friendly spirits create light, floating jumps for kids to enjoy.

  • Wisp Light Maze
    A fun maze filled with glowing lights that guide children through twisting paths.

  • Shadow Slide
    Slides surrounded by moving shadows that shift and dance along the walls.

  • Mini Polter Play
    A playful zone where small objects gently move and react, creating harmless surprises.

It’s a place where fun meets the mysterious—perfectly safe, yet just a little bit spooky.
